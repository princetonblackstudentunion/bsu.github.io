Frantzesca M. Barron - President

Frantzesca Barron is a Haitian-American rising Senior from East Orange, NJ. She is majoring in Economics with a certificate in Statistics and Machine Learning. She is also involved with the American Whig-Cliosophic Society, the first-generation low-income community, DoroBucci Dance Company, and mentoring her local robotics teams. She is hoping to bring the Black Student Union back to its roots and revitalize the Black Princeton community. Fun Fact: She has a pet turtle named Dylan.
