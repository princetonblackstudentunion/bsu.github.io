About Us


Our Mission     Board     Expectations

The purpose of the Black Student Union is to promote and to sustain an atmosphere that is conductive to the political, cultural, social, professional, and educational growth and success of the African diasporic community as well as foster a sense of community among black students at Princeton. Such an atmosphere and community can be best achieved and sustained through the pursuit, discussion, and application of knowledge, pro-active leadership, and social, political, and cultural engagement within the Black community. The Black Student Union does not represent a shared political ideology, but rather a shared experience and concern with the Black experience at Princeton University.

Preamble

We, the Black students of Princeton University, conscious of our responsibility as students to harness our resources and in hopes of strengthening our community politically, professionally, culturally, socially, and educationally, establish the Black Student Union. We are determined to consistently promote and defend the interests, integrity, and well-being of all students of the African diaspora on this campus and those of our people in the larger community.
